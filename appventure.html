Our Challenge

The goal was to support Ithaca Soap's mission of environmental transparency and product education while delivering a modern, user-friendly mobile experience. This required prioritizing usability, discoverability, and emotional connection in the design.

Design Process

  • Interviews: We conducted 4 interviews and spoke to the founder to understand both brand goals and user concerns.
  • Personas & Flows: Created personas prioritizing sustainability, budget, and skin sensitivity. Designed flows for shopping, filtering, and educational usage tips.
  • Wireframing: Iterated on Home, Products, FAQ, and Rewards pages with a mobile-first layout and cards-based hierarchy.
  • Development: Built a Vue.js PWA using JSON fixtures. Key features include filtering, product detail pages, and a dynamic referral program.
