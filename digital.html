Telegram LiveChat

Designing a transparent, real-time messaging experience.

Try It – Type "ok" to Andres

Overview

Telegram LiveChat enables users to see each other's messages as they are typed, introducing a new layer of transparency and immediacy to digital conversations. This case study explores how user research, prototyping, and UI design came together to shape the feature.

User Research Insights

We identified gaps in personalization and transparency in messaging platforms. Users expressed a desire for more expressive and real-time interactions.

Prototyping the Experience

Low and mid-fidelity prototypes helped us test ideas quickly. We explored emoji moodboards, live chat, and story formats.

Design Strategy

We prioritized LiveChat using an impact-effort matrix and SWOT analysis. This feature aligned with user desires for fluid, transparent interactions.

Why Live Chat?

LiveChat redefines how users connect in digital environments. It minimizes waiting, improves clarity, and fosters more authentic, synchronous exchanges—like texting with transparency.

Final Thoughts

Telegram LiveChat aims to humanize digital communication through design. From concept to prototype, we anchored decisions in user empathy and forward-thinking interaction.
