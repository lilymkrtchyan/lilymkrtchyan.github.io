Work Experience: Armenian National Institute & Armenian Assembly of America

During my time with the Armenian National Institute and the Armenian Assembly of America, I contributed to several impactful projects focused on documenting and preserving Armenian history and heritage.

  • Conducted extensive research on the Armenian Genocide and the conflict in Artsakh, contributing to the documentation and preservation of critical historical narratives.
  • Played a key role in archiving historical documents and updating archives related to the Armenian Genocide and the Lachin Corridor blockade. This included work on documenting and archiving files relating to the war over the Artsakh region and the blockade of the Lachin Corridor.
  • Assisted in the development of a new website for the Armenian National Institute, ensuring accessibility to comprehensive resources for researchers, educators, and the general public.
  • Collaborated with teams in both Washington, D.C., and Yerevan, Armenia, highlighting my adaptability and commitment to cross-cultural teamwork.

This experience deepened my understanding of historical preservation, policy research, and advocacy, while fostering my skills in collaboration, organization, and attention to detail.
